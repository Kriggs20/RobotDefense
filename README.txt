RobotDefense

Author: Kyle Riggs

Outline: A program that places the user in a dungeon where they need to defend against a multitude of different robots coming to attack them

Application: This project first initializes a "dungeon" with obstructions that is displayed in the terminal. Then, the player character is initilaized where they are allowed to look and move around freely. Different types of robots are spawned in and go about their different ways to aproach and kill the player. The game continues until the player character has finally met their demise.
